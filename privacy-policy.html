Your Rights

1. Introduction

At Rizonet Consulting ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website, mobile applications, and any related services, sales, marketing, or events.

By using our services, you trust us with your personal information. We take this responsibility seriously and aim to explain in the clearest way possible what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

2. Information We Collect

In the course of providing recruitment and consulting services, we may collect the following categories of data:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, address, phone number, and email address.
  • Professional Information: Employment history, education, skills, CV/Resume details, and professional certifications.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, and device information collected through cookies.
  • Financial Data: Payroll information (only if you are placed as a contractor through our services).

3. How We Use Data

We process your information for purposes based on legitimate business interests, the fulfillment of our contract with you, compliance with our legal obligations, and/or your consent. This includes:

Recruitment Services

To match your profile with suitable career opportunities and job openings.

Communication

To send you updates regarding your applications or administrative notices.

Legal Compliance

To comply with taxation, employment, and anti-discrimination laws.

Service Improvement

To analyze website usage and improve our digital experiences.

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process.

"However, please remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal information, transmission of personal information to and from our services is at your own risk."

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., EU/UK under GDPR or California under CCPA), you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:
