1. Introduction

Rizonet Consulting ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our clients and candidates. This Opt-in Policy explains how we collect and use your contact information to communicate with you about recruitment opportunities, consulting services, and company updates.

By providing your phone number or email address through our website or during the recruitment process, you agree to receive communications as described in this policy.

2. Express Consent

We only send marketing or automated recruitment messages to individuals who have provided their express consent. Consent is obtained through:

  • Checking a dedicated box on our application forms.
  • Submitting a "Stay Updated" request via our website.
  • Verbal confirmation during an initial candidate screening call.
  • Direct email subscription to our newsletter or job alerts.

3. SMS Messaging

If you opt-in to receive SMS messages from Rizonet Consulting, please be aware of the following:

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your recruitment activity and chosen alert settings.
Rates: Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

SMS communications may include interview reminders, new job postings matching your profile, and status updates regarding your application.

4. Opt-out Instructions

You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. We make the opt-out process as simple as possible:

sms SMS

Reply STOP to any message you receive from us. You will receive one final confirmation text.

mail Email

Click the "Unsubscribe" link located at the bottom of any marketing or alert email.

Alternatively, you can email privacy@rizonet.com with the subject line "Opt-out Request" and specify which channels you wish to be removed from.

5. Third-Party Sharing

Rizonet Consulting respects your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or lease your contact information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Data may only be shared with service providers who assist us in delivering communications (e.g., SMS gateways), and they are contractually prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

Questions about this policy?

Our privacy team is here to help you understand how we protect your information.
